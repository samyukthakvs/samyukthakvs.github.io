The Reality on the Ground

Your CHRO shouldn't be running
the board on three-day-old Excel.

Every Monday, Plant HR teams rebuild the same manpower report from scratch. Every quarter, Finance wonders if the payroll numbers are real. Every compliance visit is a fire drill. There's a better way.

⚠️

Compliance is a Permanent Fire Drill

Labour Inspector walks in unannounced. Form 13–23 are incomplete. Contractors' PF challans haven't been submitted by three vendors. Your Plant HR manager spends a full day assembling what should be real-time.

"It would take at least a day, and they still wouldn't all be complete."
💸

Payroll Leakage You Can't See

Ghost workers on the biometric register who aren't on the floor. Night shift allowances applied to day workers. Overtime manipulation across plants. Finance has never audited biometric-to-payroll accuracy — because there's no tool to do it.

"OT is a big line item, but we've never specifically audited the link to attendance."
📊

No Single View Across Plants

Attrition data from Pune. Headcount from Manesar. Compliance scorecard from Chennai — all in separate files sent by plant HR teams on different days. By the time the CHRO consolidates it for the board, it's stale and incomplete.

"It takes 3–4 days to pull together. It's never fully accurate."

Discovery Playbook

The questions that open every deal.

Use these verbatim. Each one is designed to surface a specific pain — and create a specific entry point.

🔴 CHRO — Board Pressure

"When your MD or board asks for a workforce snapshot across all plants — what does that process look like today? How much of it is Excel?"

🟠 Plant HR — Monday Morning

"Walk me through your Monday morning — from when you arrive, to when you send the manpower report to Production. What does that look like?"

🟠 Plant HR — Inspector Readiness

"When a Labour Inspector walks in unannounced, how long does it take you to pull together all the CLRA registers for your contractors?"

🟡 CFO — OT Leakage

"Do you have a sense of what overtime spend looks like as a percentage of your wage bill? Has it ever been audited for accuracy?"

🔵 Plant Head / COO — Production Impact

"How often does a production line get delayed because of a manpower shortage you didn't see coming? What does that cost per hour?"

🔴 ALL — The Golden Question

"If I asked your Plant HR Manager right now how ready they are for an unannounced Labour Inspector visit — what do you think they'd say?"
